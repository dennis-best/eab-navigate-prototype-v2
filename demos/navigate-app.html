Student Campaigns

Student Campaigns are campaigns that can be sent directly to the students to complete a specific action or to be notified at different times.

Appointment Campaigns

Allows staff to reach out to specific student populations and encourage them to schedule appointments. Appointment Campaigns are best deployed by staff members seeking to encourage students to meet with them for advising or other services.

Add New

Messaging Campaigns

Email or text a group of students on specified dates about certain things they may need to do or attend.

Add New

Allows staff to reach out to specific student populations and encourage them to enroll in a specific term. Enrollment Campaigns are best deployed by staff members seeking to encourage students to enroll in an upcoming term.

Add New

Survey Campaigns

Send out surveys for students to fill out and keep track of their progress in answering the survey.

Add New
Staff Campaigns

Staff Campaigns are campaigns that can be sent directly to Staff users to complete a specific action and track outcomes for each campaign.

Progress Report Campaigns

Progress Report Campaigns identify potential barriers to student success through instructor feedback. Progress Report Campaigns are initiated by staff in order to collect information from instructors.

Add New

Enrollment Census Campaigns

Enrollment Censuses let you monitor student attendance patterns, including which students attended class and the date of their last attendance.

Add New

Travel Letters

Travel Letters notify professors about course meetings students will miss due to travel for activities such as sporting events, on-campus mentoring groups, and/or student government trips or meetings.
